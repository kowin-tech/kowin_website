About Us

Kowin Tech is a dynamic information and communications technology (ICT) solutions provider. We have the distinction of being formed and managed by skilled IT professionals with the commitment and experience that permits no compromise of quality to our products and services.

Expertise in establishing process and enabling IT and Telecommunication across Financial Services, IT, Retail, and BPO industries.

We offer a wide range of IT and ICT solutions including fully managed IT support and consultancy services, hosted cloud solutions, VoIP telephony, dialler management services, reporting & analytics.
