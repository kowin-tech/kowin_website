Reporting & Analytics

Got data, but not sure how good it is? Let us help you!

In order to get the best results, we offer a cell phone and data cleaning service. This means that any data that is sent or uploaded for campaigns, will be run through a database that will verify and rank these leads according to quality. This information can be utilised accordingly in your call centre and your AVM campaigns!

Along with data cleaning, we offer services that give you real-time analytics and feedback on how your information is performing.

This will be populated in reports as well as customised dashboards that allow you to accurately track your current performance and adjust your strategies accordingly to ensure you achieve your KPIs. Real-time feedback is essential to your business' success!

So, how exactly does data cleaning work? Have a look below!

1

Your information is uploaded to our data cleaning platform.

2

The system runs a real-time analysis by calling each of the numbers in your list.

3

The system then ranks them accordingly eg. Rank 8 = Client answers, high quality lead.

4

This is then formulated on a report.

5

We export this report.

6

It's imported into your system.

7

The leads are registered in your campaign, and you are ready to call!
