Seamless Integration with Your Tools

Kowin Tech's system is built to integrate smoothly with external tools and workflows, ensuring your dialer works in harmony with your business processes. Key integration features include:

Webhook on Disposition (Dispo Call URL)

Automatically trigger external webhooks when a call is finished and an outcome is selected. This means every time an agent dispositions a call, the system can instantly send the results (e.g. survey answers, call outcome, agent notes) to your external CRM, database, or application. No manual exports – data flows in real-time to where you need it.

Start Call URL

Kick off external actions at the moment a call connects. Our platform can call a specified URL as soon as a call is sent to an agent, allowing you to log call events or fetch customer data from an external system right at call start. This helps in syncing live call information with third-party systems or initiating related workflows.

Agent Webform Button

Equip agents with a one-click webform button that opens a custom web page or CRM screen pre-filled with the customer's information. Agents can update records, fill out survey forms, or view client history without wasting time searching—boosting efficiency and accuracy during calls.

iFrame Embedding

Embed external web pages directly into the agent interface. For example, agents can view or input data into your existing survey application or CRM inside the call script window, all within the Vicidial agent screen. This integrated desktop experience means agents don't juggle multiple applications; everything they need is in one place.

Open API & Custom Integration

(Optional) In addition to built-in features, our Vicidial-based solution supports API integration and customization. We can work with your technical team to connect other tools or build bespoke integrations so that the dialer fits perfectly into your ecosystem.

Bottom line:

We eliminate data silos by making sure call data and external systems talk to each other seamlessly. Your outbound survey campaigns will not only collect valuable data but also automatically push that data to your analytics tools, CRM, or any platform of your choice in real time.

Voice Broadcast & Lead Nurturing

Leverage voice broadcasting to amplify your reach and prepare your leads for live interaction. This feature allows you to send a pre-recorded voice message to a large list of contacts, which is ideal for initial engagement and cleaning up lead lists:

Initial Engagement

Quickly broadcast a survey invitation or important announcement to thousands of contacts. Recipients can be prompted to press a key (e.g. "Press 1 to participate in the survey") which will automatically route interested respondents to an available agent or an interactive voice response (IVR) survey. This ensures your agents spend time only with contacts who have expressed interest, boosting conversion rates.

Scrubbing Old Leads

Use voice broadcasts to scrub older or unresponsive leads. By calling with a recorded message, you can detect which numbers are no longer in service, which go to voicemail, and which still reach a live person. This process cleans your lists, improves data quality, and increases future contactability by focusing on valid numbers.

High Efficiency Outreach

One agent or campaign manager can initiate a voice broadcast that touches all contacts in a list without one-by-one dialing. The system handles the heavy lifting – dialing and playing messages – while your team monitors results and handles callbacks or live transfers from interested contacts.

Compliance and Personalization

Our system can throttle call volume and insert compliance-safe options (like DNC opt-outs or safe harbor messages for drops) to adhere to regulations. You can also personalize broadcasts by region or list, and even localize the caller ID (CLI) so recipients see a familiar area code, increasing the likelihood they'll answer the call.

By using voice broadcasts as a strategic first touch, you not only engage a broad audience quickly but also refine your lead pool for subsequent live calling or follow-up campaigns.

Inbound Blending for Unified Campaigns

Modern contact centers need to handle both outbound and inbound communication without missing a beat. Kowin Tech's Vicidial system supports blended campaigns, allowing your team to manage incoming calls while running outbound surveys, all in one unified platform:

Seamless Switch Between Outbound and Inbound

Agents can be configured to handle outbound survey calls and automatically receive inbound calls (such as callbacks or incoming inquiries) in the same session. If your marketing campaign prompts customers to call back, those calls can be routed to the same agents conducting outbound calls, ensuring a consistent experience.

Priority Routing and Availability

You can prioritize inbound calls so that when the phone rings, available agents immediately get the call. The system will momentarily pause outbound dialing for that agent, then resume once the inbound call is handled. This maximizes productivity while guaranteeing prompt attention to inbound leads.

Unified Agent Desktop

Whether it's an incoming customer call or an outbound survey, the agent uses the same interface and has access to the same customer data and scripts. This unified approach reduces confusion and training time, and it helps agents provide better service since all context is in one place.

Balanced Workloads

Blending helps keep agents engaged at all times – when inbound volume is low, outbound dialing fills the gaps; when inbound spikes, the system adjusts. This results in improved service levels for inbound calls and higher efficiency for outbound operations, without needing separate teams or systems.

Better Customer Experience

If a contact from your survey list calls back (maybe from a missed call or after receiving a voice broadcast or SMS), the blended system can recognize the number and, if desired, route them to the appropriate campaign or agent group. The agent can greet them knowing they responded to the survey outreach, creating a smoother customer journey.

Our inbound/outbound blending capability ensures you never miss opportunities and maintain optimal productivity, all within a single, easy-to-manage campaign setup.

Advanced Dialing Strategies

Our experts will implement and advise on proven dialing tactics to boost contact and conversion rates:

Localized Caller ID (CLI)

Increase answer rates by displaying a local or recognizable number to each contact. We can dynamically match area codes or use regional numbers when calling different locales, making recipients more likely to pick up a call from a familiar-looking number.

Multi-CLI Rotation

Protect your outbound numbers' reputation by rotating through a pool of caller IDs. This technique reduces the risk of any single number being flagged as spam. It helps combat spam detection apps and keeps your connect rates high over the long run.

Combatting Truecaller & Spam Labels

We proactively manage your caller IDs to avoid detection and blocking by apps like Truecaller and carrier spam filters. Our strategy includes monitoring for flagged numbers, refreshing or registering numbers as needed, and varying call patterns to minimize the chance of being marked as spam. The goal is to ensure your calls actually reach the prospects instead of getting filtered out.

Optimal Call Timing & Pacing

We'll help configure dialing schedules and pacing that respect time zones and optimal call times for your target audience, while staying compliant with regulations. This includes controlling drop call ratios and call abandonment to meet standards.
